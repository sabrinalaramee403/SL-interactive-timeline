reputation

a timeline of Taylor Swift's greatest era

Reputation. The word to describe Taylor Swift’s greatest and most detailed era. From beginning to end, the era was intricate, intimate, and inspiring. Taylor Swift showed that she came out stronger after taking a brief hiatus.

8.18.2017

accounts wiped

Taylor’s social media accounts were completely wiped of photos, posts, and who she was following.

8.21.2017

cryptic snake video

A few days later, a cryptic snake video was posted on all her social media accounts. The snake slithers and jumps to “bite” the screen.

8.24.2017

"look what you made me do"

The era’s first single is released, along with a music video filled with snakes and references to all the people that have tried to bring her down.

10.19.2017

"gorgeous"

The next song of the era is released, but there was no music video. It wasn’t released as a single, but just a song off the album.

10.27.2017

"...ready for it?"

The second official single of the era is released along with a music video.

11.2.2017

"call it what you want"

The next song of the era is released, and like “Gorgeous”, there was no music video.

11.10.2017

album release

Reputation is released, and becomes very successful in its first week.

12.1.2017

first rep performance

Taylor Swift’s first performance of the era for kiis fm jingle ball.

3.11.2018

"delicate"

The third official single of the era is released with a music video that represents the world not seeing Taylor being her true self.

5.8.2018

reputation stadium tour starts

The tour begins in Glendale, Arizona.

5.18.2018

shawn mendes

Shawn Mendes is the first guest of the tour, and they performed “There’s No Holding Me Back.”

5.19.2018

selena gomez

Selena Gomez is the second guest, who made a very beautiful speech about Taylor after they performed “Hands to Myself.”

5.19.2018

troye sivan

Troye Sivan was the third guest, and the second of that night's show. He and Taylor performed “My, My, My.”

6.22.2018

niall horan

Ex-One Direction member Niall Horan appears as the fourth guest. He and Taylor performed “Slow Hands.”

6.23.2018

robbie williams

Taylor began performing Robbie’s song “Angels”, and he joined in halfway through the song, making him the fifth guest of the tour.

7.26.2018

hayley kiyoko

Hayley Kiyoko is the sixth guest, who performed her song “Curious” alongside Taylor.

8.4.2018

bryan adams

Bryan Adams joins Taylor on stage as the seventh guest, performing “Summer of ‘69” with her.

8.25.2018

tim mcgraw & faith hill

Country couple Tim McGraw and Faith Hill join Taylor to perform “Tim McGraw.” They were the eighth guest on the tour.

10.5.2018

maren morris

Maren Morris joins Taylor on stage to perform her & Zedd’s song, “The Middle.”

10.7.2018

sugarland

Sugarland pairs up with Taylor to perform their single, “Babe”, together for the first time.

11.21.2018

reputation stadium tour ends

The tour ends in Tokyo, Japan.

12.31.2018

reputation netflix special

A video recording of the show is released on Netflix, and does not include any of the special guests or surprise songs that Taylor performed on the tour.

1.28.2019

last post before the ts7 era

Taylor’s last social media post of the Reputation era showed a picture of her in her trailer during the filming for “Cats.”
